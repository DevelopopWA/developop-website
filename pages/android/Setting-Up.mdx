# Android Studio Installation Guide

## Step 1
### All Devices
Install Android Studio on your machine from Android Developer Site:  
Go to https://developer.android.com/studio  
Press Download Android Studio Electric Eel.  
Scroll/read and agree to the terms and conditions, and the installation should start.  
## Step 2
### Mac
Once scrolled to the bottom, there is a choice between Mac with intel chip and Mac with Apple chip. To check what chip is in your Mac, go over to top left corner of menu bar and click on Apple logo. On the drop-down menu, click about this mac, which should open a window that displays the type of chip in the machine. After determining what kind of chip your Mac has, select the choice accordingly.  
Installation should be in .DMG format inside your Downloads folder
